About

Why did we start Tech Finder UK?

Simply - We love tech! However finding the right gadget is sometimes expensive! As a person trying to study or creating thier own business, small tech gadgets can not only help them save money but time!

When browsing on the web for a specific item can be hard as there are many brands nowadays! It's hard to tell if a certain product is any good too!

Therefore we created this website dedicated to find cheaper gadgets to help work flow, improve gaming or day to day lifestyle!

Let us know if there's any products you would want us to test out!

FAQ

Question 1: Are our posts sponsored?

Nope! Our products are purchased with our own money so our thoughts and opinions are ours! If we purchased a product we don’t like, we’ll let you know!

Question 2: I want to submit a product for review? How can I contact you?

We love to hear great finds from the community! Please pop us a message at techfinderuk@gmail.com or use the Contact Us page and we’ll get back to you shortly!

Question 3: I can’t find your prices that you advertised?

The prices in our reviews are just a guide, and the current price may fluctuate. We will try to give an estimate of costs so you know what is a reasonable price for a product.

Question 4: There’s no links to your products, how do I find them?

We are not affiliated with any company there for we will not use affiliated links. The products can be usually found through standard websites or through a search engine.

Question 5: The product is no longer available.

The products may be discontinued and no longer be on sale. However we try our best to find available tech so it’s easier to find!

Question 6: I am trying to purchase a new laptop for school, but I am not sure which one to purchase, can you advise?

Sure! Our customer service team will gladly help you! You can message them on any social media or through our email at techfinderuk@gmail.com.
